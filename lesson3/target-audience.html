Target Audience

The target audience is described below:

  • Who: Everyone has a reason to check the weather :) Because the audience is so broad, the interface will need to be friendly to all people even those who may be technologically challenged.
  • Age: 12+
  • Motivation: These are people looking to be prepared for whatever the weather throws at them. People are looking to plan their day or weekend according to the forecasted weather.

Personas

Charlie Stone

  • Demographics: 16 year old high school student, works at Hobby Lobby, 2nd oldest in the family
  • Goals and motivations for using the site: Check the weather to know what shoes and clothes to wear before driving to school.
  • Technology: Uses an iPhone and does school tasks on his parents Mac.

Scenarios

  1. What is the weather going to be like tonight? Will I have to shovel snow in the morning?
  2. What will the weather be like this weekend? Billy has a baseball game that might get rained out
  3. Will it be snowy and windy on i80 in Wyoming this weekend? I need to get to Denver for a wedding.
  4. Last year graduation was wet and cold. Will it be the same thing tomorrow for my grad party?
  5. I wonder how cold it is in Norway today.
