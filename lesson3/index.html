Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to quickly and fully inform visitors what the weather will be like for that day. It will get the user the actionable insights with no delay and with as much detail as possible without sacrificing speed. A secondary purpose or goal would be provide visitors with a look towards the near future so that they can make plans according to the information that they find on the site.
